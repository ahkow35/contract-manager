Watermark style for free exports

How should the logo appear on documents exported by free-tier users?

HighlightEdit

A — Diagonal Text

Classic watermark pattern. "HighlightEdit" rotated across the page at low opacity. Familiar, hard to miss, and clearly an incentive to upgrade. Used by DocuSign, HelloSign.

Created with HighlightEdit · Free Plan · highlightedit.com

B — Footer Stamp

A thin branded footer bar on every page — logo + "Created with HighlightEdit · Free Plan". Non-intrusive, keeps the document readable. Also works as passive marketing when shared.

HighlightEdit

C — Corner Mark

Small logo + name in the bottom-right corner at low opacity. The most subtle option — barely noticeable on screen but present on print. Least friction for free users, least conversion pressure.

My recommendation: B — Footer Stamp. It's professional-looking (not defacing the document like a diagonal mark), stays out of the content area, and works as organic marketing when HR/Legal teams share documents with each other.
